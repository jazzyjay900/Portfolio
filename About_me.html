About Me

in Leeds of the same parish. I was a very energetic kid growing up
which lead to me getting in trouble with my mother very often. I attended Mt. Osborn Primary in my youth before graduating to attent St.Elizabth Technical High School (S.T.E.T.H.S) It wasn't easy growing up because
we didnt have alot of money so life became really hard really fast but I kept going with the encouragement from my family until I made it to university where I am pursuing my bachelors in computer science to hopefully become a web Developer for a massive tech company (or start my own)
